Explanations

  1. Always keep the face of the emoji

    The app takes a photo every 300ms so you have to keep the face of the emoji constantly

  2. Move !

    The goal is to have lots of different photos so go forward, backward, go left, go right, etc!

    Don't stay in one place for too long

  3. 3 second countdown!

    When you will click on Start, a 3 second countdown will take place before starting to take photos
